There is an article about range voting on wikipedia.
In simple terms, the idea is that any election winner should be average best choice for everyone.
For 4 candidates one vote:
  1. 1st will get 4 points
  2. 2nd will get 3 points
  3. 3rd will get 2 points
  4. 4th will get 1 point
And when we count all points from all votes for each candidate, the one who will get most will be the winner. And that candidate will not be hated by half of the nation.
